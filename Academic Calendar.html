Volant University's academic calendar unfolds in three vibrant terms, each marking a distinctive phase of learning and growth. The Autumn Term, commencing in September, sets the scholarly tone with a myriad of educational pursuits. Following this, the Spring Term ushers in the new year, fostering academic exploration until March. Finally, the Summer Term, starting in April, encapsulates the culmination of endeavors and heralds a season of reflection. These terms encapsulate a dynamic journey, weaving a narrative of academic rigor, personal development, and community engagement. Embracing a rich tapestry of experiences, Volant University's academic calendar embodies a commitment to fostering knowledge and nurturing future leaders.
