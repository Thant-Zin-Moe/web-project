founder Meeting of Volant Business University

     In a momentous Founder Meeting, leaders and visionaries from Califonia University of Economy gathered to reflect upon a rich history that began in 1989. Originally established as the University of Economy, it underwent a transformative journey, evolving into the Institute of Business Career in 1995. On the pivotal date of March 3, 1998, the institution embraced a new identity as the Economic University of California.

     meeting delved into the institutional growth that followed, with a network expanding to 25 Economic Universities across the country. The founders took pride in the strategic decision made on March 3, 2000, to establish the Califonia University of Economy. This move aimed to nurture and produce exceptional business specialists, envisioned to serve as invaluable human resources on a global scale.

     The gathering became a platform for reminiscing about the milestones achieved, acknowledging the foresight that led to each transformation. The founders celebrated the legacy of their alma mater, recognizing the impact it has had on producing skilled professionals who contribute to the world's economic landscape.

     As the Founders' Summit unfolded, there was a shared sense of pride in the journey from the University of Economy to the esteemed Califonia University of Economy. The institution's commitment to excellence and its mission to shape future business leaders underscored the importance of each pivotal decision made during its evolution. The Founder Meeting served not only as a reflection on the past but also as a testament to the enduring vision that continues to propel Califonia University of Economy towards greater heights in the world of academia and business.
