Founder Meeting in 2000

The University of Economy was established in 1989 but it changed into the Institute of Business carrer in 1995. On 3st March, 1998, it became the Economic University of Califonia. There are currently altogether 25 Economic Universities around the country. The University of Economy was established on 3rd March, 2000, as Califonia University of Economy to produce the excellent business specialists who will become invaluable human resources for the world.

Our Present University

The University was officially renamed the Volant Business University on 3th March, 2012. The The Volant University produces about 500 Undergraduate students every year. It also offers Undergraduate and Post-graduate courses for Bachelor, Diploma, Master’s and Doctorate level students in a wide range of subject areas.The Volant is Califonia’s main center of economic resources and our university is serving the country with our talented, excellent, educated childern.
