Bussiness Project Competition

Dear Volant University Students,

We are thrilled to announce the launch of the inaugural Business Project Competition at Volant University! This exciting opportunity aims to unleash your creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurial spirit.

Why you participate?

Skill Development : Enhance your business acumen and project management skills.
     Networking       : Connect industry professionals and fellow students.
            Prizes         : Exciting rewards for top-performing projects.

How to participate?

1.Must be below 5 students in each team.
2.Each team must submit a detailed project proposal by the specified deadline.
3.Proposals should include a clear project description, objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes.
4.Projects must be original and not previously submitted in other competitions or academic settings.

Key Dates

Proposal Submission Deadline : 1.4.2024
       Finalists Announcement    : 5.4.2024
         Project Presentations       : 7.4.2024
Get ready to showcase your talent, innovation, and determination. This competition is not just about winning; it's about pushing your boundaries and making a lasting impact.
