Volant University Graduration

Celebrating Achievements - Volant University Graduation Ceremony 2024

Volant University proudly hosted its Graduation Ceremony on 25.2.2024, commemorating the academic achievements and perseverance of the graduating class of 2024. The ceremony, held at the Graduation Hall of Volant, filled with pride, emotions, and a sense of accomplishment.The event kicked off with a procession of graduates adorned in their academic regalia, accompanied by the traditional strains of Pomp and Circumstance. Family, friends, faculty, and distinguished guests gathered to witness the momentous occasion, celebrating the culmination of years of dedication and hard work.The Volant University Graduation Ceremony 2024 was a moment of reflection, celebration, and anticipation for the promising futures awaiting the graduates. The university extends its heartfelt congratulations to the Class of 2024, wishing them success and fulfillment in their future endeavors.
